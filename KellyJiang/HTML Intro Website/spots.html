Location Name Picture Blurb
Kinokuniya

One of my favorite spots to go with friends is called "Kinokuniya."

It's a japanese bookstore that sells a variety of products including, but are not limited to, regular books, manga, stationary, stuffed animals, figurines, etc.
Aquarium

I haven't acutally been to the aquarium in a long time, but, I remember having so much fun wandering around, looking at all the weird fish.

One of the best parts of being at an aquarium is the atmosphere, it's so calming. :-)
